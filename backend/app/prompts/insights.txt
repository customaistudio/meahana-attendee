INSIGHTS (array of strings, 2-5 items):
Surface patterns the organizer might not have noticed about their meeting leadership.

These should be genuinely useful observations, not generic advice. Look for:

Team dynamics:
- "Three team members didn't speak until directly asked - consider whether they feel safe contributing"
- "The discussion became noticeably more productive after you reframed the problem"
- "Energy dropped significantly in the second half - the group may have hit decision fatigue"

Leadership patterns:
- "You asked great open-ended questions that generated rich discussion"
- "Several ideas were raised but not acknowledged before moving on"
- "The meeting ran 15 minutes over, which may signal scope creep in your agenda"

Opportunities:
- "Alex's concern about timeline was raised twice but not fully addressed"
- "The group seemed aligned early but you continued discussing - earlier closure was possible"
- "Multiple people offered to help but no one was assigned ownership"

Each insight should help the organizer see something they couldn't see while facilitating.
