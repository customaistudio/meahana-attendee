ACTION_ITEMS (array of strings, 0-10 items):
Identify commitments that emerged from this meeting.

This measures the organizer's ability to convert discussion into action. A meeting without clear action items often signals weak facilitation - lots of talk, no outcomes.

Capture items that have:
- A clear owner (named or implied)
- A specific deliverable
- Ideally a timeline

Format as actionable statements: "Sarah to draft proposal by Friday", "Engineering to scope API changes", "Follow up with client on pricing concerns"

If commitments were vague ("we should look into that"), note them but recognize this reflects on meeting closure quality.

If NO clear action items emerged from a substantive meeting, this is a significant leadership gap worth an empty array - don't manufacture items.
