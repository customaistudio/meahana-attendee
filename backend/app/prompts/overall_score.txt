OVERALL_SCORE (float, 1.0-10.0):
This is the Meeting Leadership Score - a holistic assessment of the organizer's effectiveness at driving results.

Score based on these leadership outcomes:
- 9-10: Exceptional leadership. Clear purpose, balanced participation, decisions made, commitments secured, energy maintained. The organizer orchestrated a high-impact session.
- 7-8: Effective leadership. Meeting achieved its goals with good facilitation. Minor opportunities for improvement in engagement or closure.
- 5-6: Adequate leadership. Meeting had purpose but execution gaps - perhaps dominated by few voices, unclear outcomes, or energy dips that weren't addressed.
- 3-4: Weak leadership. Meeting lacked direction, participation was unbalanced, outcomes unclear. Organizer didn't actively shape the conversation.
- 1-2: Leadership absent. Meeting was unfocused, unproductive, or potentially harmful to team dynamics.

Be honest but constructive. A 7 is genuinely good. Reserve 9+ for truly exceptional facilitation.
