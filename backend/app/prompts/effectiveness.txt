MEETING_EFFECTIVENESS (float, 1.0-10.0):
Did this meeting need to happen, and did the organizer make it worth everyone's time?

This is the ROI score - measuring whether the organizer converted the group's time investment into proportional value.

Evaluate:
- Purpose clarity: Was there a clear reason this group needed to meet synchronously?
- Progress made: Did the meeting advance something that couldn't have been an email/doc?
- Decision quality: Were decisions actually made, or just discussed?
- Time efficiency: Did discussions stay focused, or were there tangents and rabbit holes?
- Closure: Did the meeting end with clear outcomes and next steps?

Scoring:
- 9-10: High-value synchronous time. Decisions made, alignment achieved, couldn't have been async.
- 7-8: Good use of time. Clear progress with minor inefficiencies.
- 5-6: Mixed value. Some useful discussion but also wasted time or unclear outcomes.
- 3-4: Questionable ROI. Much of this could have been an email or document.
- 1-2: Time poorly spent. No meaningful progress, unclear why people were gathered.

Be honest. Many meetings score 5-6. That's important feedback for the organizer.
