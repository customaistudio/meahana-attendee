SENTIMENT (string: "positive", "negative", or "neutral"):
Assess the emotional climate the organizer created and maintained.

This reflects the organizer's ability to set and manage the room's emotional temperature:
- "positive": The organizer fostered psychological safety. People contributed freely, built on each other's ideas, and left energized. Disagreements were productive, not personal.
- "negative": Warning sign. Tension, frustration, or disengagement dominated. The organizer either created this climate or failed to address it. May indicate people felt unheard, dismissed, or that the meeting was a waste of their time.
- "neutral": Functional but uninspiring. Business was conducted without emotional engagement. The organizer missed opportunities to energize or connect the group.

Look for: tone of responses, how disagreements are handled, whether people volunteer ideas or only speak when called upon, signs of enthusiasm or resignation.
